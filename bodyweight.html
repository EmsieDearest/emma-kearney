Bodyweight

I was among the many who had to forgo their gym membership when COVID-19 erupted. My friends know me as a bit of a gym rat, having trained through CrossFit and was on a rowing team. Working out from home was a transition to say the least, not being able to weight train the same way or fit a rowing machine inside my tiny apartment. It turned into an opportunity to explore other workouts that I didn't normally have the time to do. I got really into following workout videos of boxing, pilates, barre and yoga where you primarily use your body weight for strength training. I even started learning ballet! It was wonderful to see how you could get such a great workout without needing much equipment.

This app was designed for people who workout from home. Who don't have any equipment, or just want to level up with what they have around the house. I miss the trainers that I saw on a weekly basis. They were my friends. I wanted it to have the appeal of a trendy workout studio, with instructors who bring their authentic quirky selves to the class like when you know Fire Flow Yoga with Freya G. is going to be a blast. After class you can post a selfie of how sweaty you got and share it with Freya on Instagram, maybe even joke about how you cut up a pair of old sweatpants and used the elastic waistband for resistance.
