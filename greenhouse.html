Greenhouse App

Designed and developed for interior design lovers who incorporate plants into their aesthetic. Marketed to millennials on Instagram who are drawn to the trendy, albeit trickier to maintain, plants. The app sends reminders to users when it is time to water, feed or re-pot.

The app is sectioned into four regions. One part is the area that houses a collection curated by the user. This collection, aka "The Greenhouse", houses all the plants the user owns. Part of the onboarding process includes searching via name or description for each plant in the database. The second region is the Discover area where users can learn about other kinds of plants, their genesis and tips and tricks. The third region is a discussion board where users can ask more specific questions for plant aficionados to answer, such as uploading a photo of a fairly dead-looking plant and asking, "Is my plant worth saving?" The fourth and final region is the user's account, where they can set their reminder preferences.

This app is currently in development. When it is completed, the next step will be to run usability tests for those with and without disabilities. Making sure each interaction is fully functional and the accessibility API is fully operable on the native iOS.
