Hi. My name is Emma Kearney (she/her). I am a multi-disciplinary designer, front end developer and accessibility specialist.

I create unique digital experiences for all types of folks with all types of abilities in all types of industries. I've worked full time for startups, agencies, fortune 500s and with individual clients on a freelance basis. Let's work together.

DID YOU KNOW?

Webflow, Squarespace, WordPress and other website generators use cheap plugins as band-aid solutions to fix a whole slew of accessibility issues that prevent people with disabilities from reading content, interacting with elements and meeting universal compliance. This a big problem, but luckily it's slowly changing for the better.

While it would have been easier to use one of those generators to create my portfolio, I thought it was important for mine to be as accessible as possible. I designed this site in Figma, developed it from the ground up and programmed it to meet universal web accessibility compliance. This means my portfolio is accessible for people who are blind, visually impaired and/or have cognitive disabilities.
